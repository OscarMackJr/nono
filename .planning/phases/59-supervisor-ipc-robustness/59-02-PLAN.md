## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-59-01a | Denial of Service | macOS `run_supervisor_loop` read of a partial frame | mitigate | Wire `set_read_timeout(5s)`; `read_exact` returns `WouldBlock`/`TimedOut` instead of blocking forever. | T-59-01b | Denial of Service | reconnect thrash (child loops connect/disconnect) | mitigate | Re-accept is bounded by child liveness (`waitpid(WNOHANG)` already in the loop) + supervisor exit — reconnect cannot outlive the child; no infinite tight loop. |
| T-59-01c | Elevation / Tampering | timeout-induced fail-open | mitigate | A timed-out/partial frame is ABANDONED (fail-closed): no capability is granted on a partial/timed-out request; supervision continues with the sandbox intact (Security Domain invariant). |
